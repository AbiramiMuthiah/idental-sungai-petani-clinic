Privacy Policy

Klinik Pergigian iDental, Sungai Petani

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

When you contact us via WhatsApp or our booking system, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, and appointment preferences (service type, date, and time) in order to schedule, confirm, reschedule, or cancel your dental appointments.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

- Schedule and manage your dental appointments
- Send appointment confirmations, reminders, and updates via WhatsApp and email
- Generate invoices for completed treatments
- Respond to your questions and requests

How We Store Your Information

Your information is stored securely using industry-standard cloud database services and is only accessible to authorized clinic staff and the automated systems used to manage appointments.

Third-Party Services

We use WhatsApp Business API, Google Calendar, and Google Sheets to manage appointment scheduling and communications. These services process your information solely for the purpose of delivering our appointment and communication services to you.

Data Retention

We retain your appointment and contact information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with applicable healthcare record-keeping obligations.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us directly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Klinik Pergigian iDental, Sungai Petani.
